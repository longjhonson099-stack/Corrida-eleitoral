# Customer Support - Validations

## Helpdesk Setup Checklist

### **Id**
helpdesk-setup-checklist
### **Type**
checklist
### **Description**
Verify helpdesk is properly configured
### **Items**
  - Channels configured (email, chat, social)
  - Routing rules established
  - Business hours and SLAs set
  - Email templates configured
  - Auto-responders set up
  - Ticket fields customized
  - Views and queues created
  - Notifications configured
  - Integrations connected (CRM, product)
  - Team members added with correct roles

checklist

## Article Quality Check

### **Id**
article-quality-check
### **Type**
checklist
### **Description**
Before publishing KB article
### **Items**
  - Title clearly states topic
  - Problem/question addressed in first paragraph
  - Steps are numbered and clear
  - Screenshots/visuals current
  - Links tested and working
  - No jargon or unclear terms
  - Related articles linked
  - Keywords included for search
  - Reviewed by someone else
  - Updated date is current

## Response Quality Check

### **Id**
response-quality-check
### **Type**
checklist
### **Description**
QA checklist for agent responses
### **Items**
  - Greeting is personal and warm
  - Problem fully understood
  - Solution complete and accurate
  - Next steps clear
  - Appropriate tone for situation
  - Grammar and spelling correct
  - Relevant KB articles linked
  - Sets expectations (timeline, follow-up)
  - Closing invites further questions
  - Internal notes added for context

## Support Metrics Benchmarks

### **Id**
support-metrics-check
### **Type**
checklist
### **Description**
Weekly metrics review
### **Items**
  - First response time <1 hour (chat <5 min)
  - Resolution time within SLA
  - CSAT >90%
  - First contact resolution >70%
  - Ticket reopen rate <5%
  - AI deflection rate on target
  - Agent utilization healthy (not >80%)
  - Backlog not growing

## Escalation Readiness

### **Id**

checklist
### **Description**
Before escalating a ticket
### **Items**
