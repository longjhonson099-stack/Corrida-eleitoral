# Design - Sharp Edges

## Ai Design Sameness

### **Id**
ai-design-sameness
### **Summary**
AI designs all look similar
### **Severity**
medium
### **Tools Affected**
  - uizard
  - galileo-ai
  - canva-ai
### **Situation**
Generated designs lack brand uniqueness
### **Why**
AI trained on similar data, produces similar outputs
### **Solution**
Use AI for speed, add unique brand elements

## Image Licensing

### **Id**
image-licensing
### **Summary**
AI image licensing can be unclear
### **Severity**
high
### **Tools Affected**
  - midjourney
  - stable-diffusion
### **Situation**
Using AI images commercially
### **Why**
Training data may include copyrighted images
### **Solution**
Use Adobe Firefly or similar with clear licensing
