# Community Growth - Validations

## No Acquisition Strategy

### **Id**
no-acquisition-strategy
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Should have defined member acquisition channels
### **Indicators**
  - No idea where members come from
  - Random growth
  - No tracking of sources
### **Message**
No structured acquisition strategy.
### **Fix Action**
Define and track acquisition channels with goals for each
