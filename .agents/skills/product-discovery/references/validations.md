# Product Discovery - Validations

## Building Without Customer Input

### **Id**
no-customer-interviews
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Major features should have customer research backing
### **Indicators**
  - No interviews before building
  - Only internal assumptions
### **Message**
Building without customer discovery.
### **Fix Action**
Conduct 5-10 customer interviews before committing to build

## Leading Interview Questions

### **Id**
leading-questions
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Interview questions should be open and neutral
### **Indicators**
  - Would you use a product that...
  - Don't you think...
### **Message**
Interview questions may be leading.
### **Fix Action**
Rewrite as open-ended, past-focused questions

## Seeking Only Confirmation

### **Id**
confirmation-only
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Discovery should seek disconfirming evidence
### **Indicators**
  - Only talking to friendly users
  - All feedback positive
### **Message**
Discovery may suffer from confirmation bias.
### **Fix Action**
Actively seek disconfirming evidence and critical users

## No Continuous Discovery

### **Id**
no-discovery-cadence
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Discovery should be ongoing, not a phase
### **Indicators**
  - Discovery only at project start
  - No regular customer touchpoints
### **Message**
Discovery treated as phase, not continuous practice.

Establish weekly customer touchpoints

## Solution Before Problem

### **Id**

high
### **Type**
conceptual

### **Indicators**
