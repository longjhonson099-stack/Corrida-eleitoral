# Sales AI Tools

## Patterns


---
  #### **Name**
Waterfall enrichment
  #### **Description**
Use multiple data sources in sequence for best coverage
  #### **Example**
Apollo first, Clearbit second, manual research third
  #### **Impact**
80%+ match rate vs 50-60% single source

---
  #### **Name**
Multi-channel sequences
  #### **Description**
Combine email, LinkedIn, calls in one sequence
  #### **Example**
Email Day 1, LinkedIn Day 3, Call Day 5, Email Day 7
  #### **Impact**
2-3x reply rates vs email only

---
  #### **Name**
Signal-based outreach
  #### **Description**
Trigger outreach on buying signals
  #### **Example**
Job change, funding, tech install, website visit
  #### **Impact**
10x better timing than cold spray

---
  #### **Name**
Account-based campaigns
  #### **Description**
Coordinate outreach across account stakeholders
  #### **Example**
Economic buyer + champion + end user sequenced together
  #### **Impact**
Faster deal cycles, higher win rates

---
  #### **Name**
CRM as single source of truth
  #### **Description**
All activity, data, and insights flow to CRM
  #### **Example**
Automatic activity logging, enrichment sync
  #### **Impact**
Accurate forecasting, no data silos

## Anti-Patterns


---
  #### **Name**
Spray and pray
  #### **Description**
Blasting generic emails to huge lists
  #### **Impact**
Low reply rates, domain reputation damage
  #### **Fix**
Smaller, targeted lists with personalization

---
  #### **Name**
Too many tools
  #### **Description**
Different tool for every function
  #### **Impact**
Data silos, inefficiency, high cost
  #### **Fix**
Choose integrated stack, minimize point solutions

---
  #### **Name**
Ignoring deliverability
  #### **Description**
Sending cold email without warmup
  #### **Impact**
Emails go to spam, domain blacklisted
  #### **Fix**
Warm up domains, monitor deliverability, use tools

---
  #### **Name**
No activity tracking
  #### **Description**
Failing to log calls, emails, meetings
  #### **Impact**
No visibility, poor forecasting
  #### **Fix**
Auto-logging, CRM discipline

---
  #### **Name**
Buying data without process
  #### **Description**
Purchasing ZoomInfo without knowing how to use it
  #### **Impact**
Expensive shelfware
  #### **Fix**
Define workflows before buying data
