# AI Trend Alchemy

## Patterns


---
  #### **Name**
The Signal Detection Stack
  #### **Description**
Multi-layer system for detecting emerging trends
  #### **When**
Building or operating trend detection capability
  #### **Example**
    DETECTION LAYERS:
    
    LAYER 1: QUANTITATIVE SIGNALS
    - Google Trends velocity (not just volume)
    - Social mention acceleration
    - Search volume changes
    - Hashtag emergence
    Tools: Google Trends, Exploding Topics, SparkToro
    
    LAYER 2: COMMUNITY SIGNALS
    - Reddit new topic emergence
    - Discord server activity
    - Niche forum discussions
    - Creator content shifts
    Method: Manual monitoring + alerts
    
    LAYER 3: CREATOR SIGNALS
    - What are early-adopter creators making?
    - What are they talking about?
    - What formats are they experimenting with?
    Method: Curated creator list, daily review
    
    LAYER 4: CULTURAL SIGNALS
    - What's the mood?
    - What tensions are building?
    - What's about to be memed?
    Method: Intuition + pattern recognition
    
    INTEGRATION:
    - Weighted scoring across layers
    - Trend radar updated daily
    - Priority queue for content team
    

---
  #### **Name**
Trend Timing Framework
  #### **Description**
Identify optimal entry point for trend content
  #### **When**
Deciding when to create content for a trend
  #### **Example**
    TREND LIFECYCLE:
    
    1. EMERGENCE (early signal)
       - Small communities discussing
       - Early creators experimenting
       - Search/social data starting to move
       → Best time for trend CREATION content
       → Risky: might not take off
    
    2. GROWTH (acceleration)
       - Mainstream creators joining
       - Media starting to cover
       - Clear upward trajectory
       → Best time for RIDING content
       → Sweet spot: high potential, not yet saturated
    
    3. PEAK (maximum attention)
       - Everyone is talking about it
       - Mainstream media coverage
       - Brands jumping in
       → Too late for most content
       → Exception: truly unique angle
    
    4. DECAY (declining relevance)
       - Fatigue setting in
       - "Cringe" label appearing
       - Engagement dropping
       → Avoid unless subverting expectation
    
    TIMING DECISION:
    - Can we be in GROWTH phase? → GO
    - Are we entering PEAK? → Only with unique angle
    - Are we POST-PEAK? → Skip, wait for next trend
    

---
  #### **Name**
Trend-Content Generation Loop
  #### **Description**
Rapid cycle from signal to content
  #### **When**
Executing on a detected trend
  #### **Example**
    THE LOOP (target: 2-4 hours):
    
    HOUR 1: SIGNAL TO CONCEPT
    - Trend detected/confirmed
    - Brand relevance assessed
    - 3 concept angles generated
    - Best angle selected
    
    HOUR 2: CONCEPT TO CONTENT
    - AI image generation (5-10 variants)
    - AI copy generation
    - Select and refine best combination
    - Format for platforms
    
    HOUR 3: CONTENT TO REVIEW
    - Brand safety check
    - Cultural sensitivity check
    - Quality review
    - Final polish
    
    HOUR 4: REVIEW TO LIVE
    - Platform upload
    - Scheduling/posting
    - Initial monitoring
    - Engagement response
    
    ACCELERATION ENABLERS:
    - Pre-approved templates
    - Brand asset library
    - Rapid approval workflow
    - AI generation speed
    

---
  #### **Name**
Trend Creation Strategy
  #### **Description**
Engineer trends rather than just catching them
  #### **When**
Brand has enough reach to seed trends
  #### **Example**
    TREND CREATION ELEMENTS:
    
    1. MEMETIC HOOK:
       - Replicable format
       - Easy to participate
       - Invites variation
       Example: Challenge format, template, format
    
    2. CULTURAL RESONANCE:
       - Taps existing tension or desire
       - Says what people are thinking
       - Validates a feeling
    
    3. PARTICIPATION INCENTIVE:
       - Social currency for participating
       - Makes participants look good
       - Community belonging
    
    4. SEEDING STRATEGY:
       - Seed with creators (micro-influencers first)
       - Multiple entry points
       - Cross-platform presence
       - Don't reveal brand origin initially (if appropriate)
    
    5. AMPLIFICATION:
       - Engage with early adopters
       - Share and celebrate participation
       - Add fuel to organic fire
    
    REALITY CHECK:
    - Most trend creation attempts fail
    - Success requires significant reach OR perfect timing
    - Organic trends have advantage over manufactured
    - But: manufactured trends that succeed are extremely valuable
    

---
  #### **Name**
Cultural Sensitivity at Speed
  #### **Description**
Rapid assessment of trend appropriateness
  #### **When**
Moving fast on trends without causing harm
  #### **Example**
    SPEED-SENSITIVE CHECKLIST:
    
    Before engaging with any trend, ask:
    
    □ ORIGIN: Do we understand where this came from?
       - Some trends have problematic origins
       - Memes can mean different things to different groups
       - If unsure of origin, research or skip
    
    □ COMMUNITIES: Who claims this trend?
       - Is it associated with specific communities?
       - Would our participation feel like appropriation?
       - Are we amplifying the right voices?
    
    □ CONTEXT: Is there broader context we're missing?
       - Is this trend happening alongside tragedy/crisis?
       - Could timing make this insensitive?
       - What else is in the news?
    
    □ INTERPRETATION: How could this be misread?
       - Worst-case interpretation?
       - How would critics frame it?
       - Any double meanings?
    
    □ BRAND FIT: Does our brand have standing here?
       - Is this our lane?
       - Will it feel authentic or forced?
       - Do we add value or just extract attention?
    
    RULE: If any answer is "not sure," get another opinion.
    Speed doesn't excuse harm.
    

## Anti-Patterns


---
  #### **Name**
Trend-Chasing Exhaustion
  #### **Description**
Trying to catch every trend
  #### **Why**
Exhausts team, dilutes brand, most attempts fail
  #### **Instead**
Selective excellence. 1 great trend execution > 10 mediocre.

---
  #### **Name**
Analysis Paralysis
  #### **Description**
Over-analyzing trends until they pass
  #### **Why**
Trends have short windows; slow analysis = missed opportunity
  #### **Instead**
Decision framework. Quick go/no-go. Act or skip.

---
  #### **Name**
Force-Fitting Brand
  #### **Description**
Shoehorning brand into trends that don't fit
  #### **Why**
Forced connections look desperate; audience sees through it
  #### **Instead**
Skip trends that don't fit naturally. Wait for better fits.

---
  #### **Name**
Late to Party Pride
  #### **Description**
Publishing trend content when trend is declining
  #### **Why**
Late trend content looks out of touch
  #### **Instead**
If you missed it, skip it. Don't chase declining trends.

---
  #### **Name**
Ignoring Context
  #### **Description**
Jumping on trends without understanding broader situation
  #### **Why**
Can create PR disasters if trend relates to sensitive topics
  #### **Instead**
Context check before every trend engagement.

---
  #### **Name**
Zero-Sum Thinking
  #### **Description**
Viewing trend-catching as competition with other brands
  #### **Why**
Multiple brands can succeed with same trend if angles differ
  #### **Instead**
Find unique angle. Don't just copy how others are riding it.
