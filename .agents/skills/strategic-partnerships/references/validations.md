### **Id**
no-partnership-strategy
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual

### **Message**

Define partnership strategy aligned with business goals

## Logo Over Value

### **Id**
logo-chasing
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Partnerships should deliver measurable value
### **Indicators**
  - Partnerships announced but inactive
  - No revenue or leads from partners
### **Message**
Collecting logos without creating value.
### **Fix Action**
Focus on depth with fewer partners that deliver results

## Partner Concentration Risk

### **Id**
concentration-risk
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
No single partner should dominate
### **Indicators**
  - >30% revenue from one partner
  - >50% distribution via one channel
### **Message**
Dangerous dependency on single partner.
### **Fix Action**
Diversify partner portfolio and build alternatives
