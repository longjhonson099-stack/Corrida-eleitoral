# Pitch Narrative - Validations

## Missing Opening Hook

### **Id**
no-hook
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Pitch must open with attention-grabbing hook
### **Indicators**
  - Opens with company introduction
  - Opens with 'thank you for your time'
### **Message**
Pitch opens without earning attention.
### **Fix Action**
Add opening hook: surprising stat, bold claim, or provocative question

## Solution Before Problem Established

### **Id**
solution-before-problem
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Problem must be established before solution presented
### **Message**
Pitch presents solution before establishing problem.
### **Fix Action**
Resequence: establish problem with pain and impact first

## Too Many Core Messages

### **Id**
too-many-messages
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Pitch should have one primary message
### **Indicators**
  - More than 3 core claims
  - No single memorable takeaway
### **Message**
Pitch trying to convey too many ideas.
### **Fix Action**
Identify one primary message; others support it

## Claims Without Evidence

### **Id**
no-proof-points
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Major claims must have supporting evidence
### **Indicators**
  - Claims without case studies
  - No data supporting assertions
### **Message**
Pitch makes claims without proof points.
### **Fix Action**
Add case studies, data, or credibility markers for each major claim

## Missing Clear Ask

### **Id**
no-ask
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Pitch must end with clear call to action
### **Message**
Pitch ends without clear ask or next step.
### **Fix Action**
Add specific ask with clear next step

## Not Audience-Specific

### **Id**
generic-pitch
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Pitch should be tailored to audience
### **Indicators**
  - Same pitch for all audiences
  - No reference to audience context
### **Message**
Pitch not customized for specific audience.
### **Fix Action**
Add audience-specific references and emphasis

## Pitch Exceeds Time

### **Id**
too-long
### **Severity**
low
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Pitch should fit within allocated time
### **Indicators**
  - Core pitch exceeds 10 slides
  - Speaking portion exceeds 20 minutes
### **Message**
Pitch too long for attention span.
### **Fix Action**
Cut to 10 slides, 20 minutes maximum
