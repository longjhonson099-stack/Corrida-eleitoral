# Live Education - Validations

## No Peer Learning Structure

### **Id**
no-peer-structure
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Cohort should have peer connection mechanisms
### **Indicators**
  - No buddy system
  - No pods or groups
  - Students only interact with instructor
### **Message**
Cohort lacks peer learning structures.
### **Fix Action**
Implement buddy pairs or pods with regular touchpoints

## Live Sessions Are Lecture-Heavy

### **Id**
lecture-heavy-live
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Live time should be interactive, not lecture
### **Indicators**
  - Instructor talks > 50% of live time
  - No breakouts or exercises
  - Could be a recording
### **Message**
Live sessions are too lecture-heavy.
### **Fix Action**
Restructure to 30% teaching, 70% interaction/exercises

## No Accountability System

### **Id**
no-accountability-system
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Cohort should have accountability mechanisms
### **Indicators**
  - No deadlines
  - No peer accountability
  - No progress tracking
### **Message**
Cohort lacks accountability structures.
### **Fix Action**
Add clear deadlines, buddy check-ins, and progress visibility

## All Live, No Async Content

### **Id**
no-async-component
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Should balance live with async content
### **Indicators**
  - Everything happens live
  - No pre-recorded lessons
  - Miss a call, miss the content
### **Message**
Course is too dependent on live attendance.
### **Fix Action**
Add async content layer so core learning isn't live-dependent

## No Timezone Accommodation

### **Id**
no-timezone-consideration
### **Severity**
low
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Should consider global audience timing
### **Indicators**
  - Single timezone only
  - No recordings provided
  - No alternative options
### **Message**
No accommodation for different timezones.
### **Fix Action**
Provide recordings, rotate times, or create regional options
