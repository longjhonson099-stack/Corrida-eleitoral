# Roast Writing - Validations

## Roasting Down

### **Id**
punching-down
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Roasts should punch up or sideways, never down
### **Indicators**
  - Target has less power
  - Target is vulnerable
### **Message**
Roast may be punching down.
### **Fix Action**
Verify power dynamic, only punch up

## Lacks Affection

### **Id**
mean-spirited
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Roasts should come from place of love
### **Indicators**
  - Genuine malice
  - Would target be hurt?
### **Message**
Roast seems mean-spirited, not playful.
### **Fix Action**
Ensure underlying affection, or don't roast

## Roasting from Insecurity

### **Id**
insecurity-source
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**

### **Indicators**
