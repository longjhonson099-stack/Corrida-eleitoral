# Thought Leadership - Validations

## Generic Positioning

### **Id**
no-unique-positioning
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Thought leadership must have unique angle
### **Indicators**
  - Could be said by anyone in field
  - No contrarian or unique perspective
### **Message**
Thought leadership positioning not differentiated.
### **Fix Action**
Define unique combination of expertise, perspective, and angle

## Self-Proclaimed Authority

### **Id**
self-proclaimed
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Avoid claiming thought leadership in own content
### **Indicators**
  - Describes self as 'thought leader'
  - Uses terms like 'guru' or 'expert' for self
### **Message**
Self-proclaiming thought leadership damages credibility.
### **Fix Action**
Let work speak; let others grant authority

## Generic Platitude Content

### **Id**
platitude-content
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Content should be specific and non-obvious
### **Indicators**
  - Generic business advice
  - Could apply to any company
### **Message**
Content is generic, not thought leadership.
### **Fix Action**
Add specific examples, data, or contrarian perspective

## Inconsistent Publishing

### **Id**
no-consistency
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Thought leadership requires consistent output
### **Indicators**
  - Sporadic publishing
  - Long gaps between content
### **Message**
Publishing too inconsistent to build authority.
### **Fix Action**
Set sustainable minimum cadence and maintain it

## Too Many Platforms

### **Id**
platform-sprawl
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Focus should be on 1-2 primary platforms
### **Indicators**
  - Spread across 5+ platforms
  - Shallow presence everywhere
### **Message**
Spread too thin across platforms.
### **Fix Action**
Choose primary platform and dominate it before expanding

## Claims Without Evidence

### **Id**
no-evidence
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Thought leadership claims should have backing
### **Indicators**
  - Opinions without experience backing
  - No case studies or data
### **Message**
Thought leadership needs evidence, not just opinions.
### **Fix Action**
Back claims with experience, data, or case studies
