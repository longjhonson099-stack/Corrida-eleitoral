# Ambassador Programs

## Patterns


---
  #### **Name**
Ambassador Program Design
  #### **Description**
Framework for creating effective ambassador programs
  #### **When To Use**
When launching or redesigning ambassador program
  #### **Implementation**
    ## Ambassador Program Design
    
    ### Program Goals
    | Goal Type | Example Metrics |
    |-----------|-----------------|
    | Awareness | Reach, impressions, content created |
    | Acquisition | Referrals, signups, conversions |
    | Engagement | Events hosted, questions answered |
    | Feedback | Bug reports, feature requests |
    
    ### Tier Structure
    | Tier | Requirements | Benefits |
    |------|--------------|----------|
    | Advocate | Applied, vetted | Badge, early access |
    | Ambassador | 3+ months active | Swag, direct line |
    | Champion | Top 10%, leadership | Paid, events, travel |
    
    ### Ambassador Responsibilities
    - Create content (X pieces/month)
    - Answer community questions
    - Represent at events (optional)
    - Provide product feedback
    - Uphold code of conduct
    
    ### Company Responsibilities
    - Enablement and training
    - Exclusive access and info
    - Recognition and rewards
    - Direct communication channel
    - Support their success
    

---
  #### **Name**
Ambassador Recruitment
  #### **Description**
Finding and vetting the right ambassadors
  #### **When To Use**
When recruiting ambassadors
  #### **Implementation**
    ## Ambassador Recruitment
    
    ### Finding Candidates
    - Most active community members
    - Quality content creators
    - Helpful forum responders
    - Event organizers
    - Existing power users
    
    ### Application Process
    1. Open application form
    2. Community manager review
    3. Check existing activity
    4. Interview top candidates
    5. Trial period (1 month)
    6. Full onboarding
    
    ### Application Questions
    - Why do you want to be an ambassador?
    - What content/activities will you create?
    - How do you currently use [product]?
    - Link to existing community contributions
    - Time commitment available?
    
    ### Red Flags
    - Only want free stuff
    - No existing community presence
    - Can't articulate genuine interest
    - History of community issues
    - Unrealistic commitments
    
    ### Green Flags
    - Already helping others
    - Authentic enthusiasm
    - Quality over quantity
    - Aligns with values
    - Realistic about time
    

---
  #### **Name**
Ambassador Enablement
  #### **Description**
Setting ambassadors up for success
  #### **When To Use**
When onboarding and supporting ambassadors
  #### **Implementation**
    ## Ambassador Enablement
    
    ### Onboarding (First 30 Days)
    | Week | Focus |
    |------|-------|
    | 1 | Welcome, introductions, access setup |
    | 2 | Training on product, messaging |
    | 3 | First activity/content piece |
    | 4 | Feedback session, plan next quarter |
    
    ### Resources to Provide
    - Brand guidelines and assets
    - Messaging playbook
    - Content templates
    - Product roadmap (NDA'd)
    - Direct Slack/Discord channel
    - Ambassador-only events
    
    ### Ongoing Support
    - Monthly group calls
    - Quarterly 1:1s with top ambassadors
    - Rapid response to their needs
    - Recognition in public channels
    - Feedback loop to product team
    
    ### Content Support
    - Review their content before publish
    - Amplify through official channels
    - Provide exclusive announcements
    - Co-create flagship content
    

---
  #### **Name**
Ambassador Performance Management
  #### **Description**
Tracking and rewarding ambassador performance
  #### **When To Use**
When managing active ambassador program
  #### **Implementation**
    ## Ambassador Performance Management
    
    ### Tracking Metrics
    | Activity | Points | How Tracked |
    |----------|--------|-------------|
    | Blog post | 10 | Submitted link |
    | Social post | 2 | Tracked hashtag |
    | Event talk | 15 | Submitted proof |
    | Forum answer | 1 | Tracked in forum |
    | Bug report | 5 | GitHub issue |
    | Referral | 20 | Referral code |
    
    ### Recognition Tiers
    | Quarterly Points | Recognition |
    |------------------|-------------|
    | 50+ | Bronze - swag |
    | 100+ | Silver - premium swag + feature |
    | 200+ | Gold - conference ticket |
    | 300+ | Platinum - paid opportunity |
    
    ### Performance Reviews
    - Quarterly activity review
    - Annual program fit review
    - Graceful exit for inactive
    - Promotion path for stars
    
    ### Rewards That Work
    - Early access (costs nothing, high value)
    - Exclusive swag (not just branded junk)
    - Conference tickets and travel
    - Direct access to team
    - Paid opportunities
    - Public recognition
    

## Anti-Patterns


---
  #### **Name**
Free Labor Disguised as Ambassador Program
  #### **Description**
Expecting work without fair value exchange
  #### **Why Bad**
    Ambassadors feel used.
    Best people leave.
    Reputation damage.
    Attracts wrong people (freebie hunters).
    
  #### **What To Do Instead**
    Genuine value exchange.
    Clear what ambassadors get.
    Don't oversell benefits.
    Pay for real work.
    

---
  #### **Name**
Set It and Forget It
  #### **Description**
Launching program then not managing it
  #### **Why Bad**
    Ambassadors disengage.
    No accountability.
    Program dies quietly.
    
  #### **What To Do Instead**
    Dedicated program manager.
    Regular engagement.
    Active community.
    Continuous improvement.
    

---
  #### **Name**
Too Many Ambassadors
  #### **Description**
Accepting everyone to inflate numbers
  #### **Why Bad**
    Dilutes meaning.
    Can't support everyone.
    Quality drops.
    Ambassadors feel unspecial.
    
  #### **What To Do Instead**
    Cap program size.
    Maintain quality bar.
    Waitlist if needed.
    Tiers for scale.
