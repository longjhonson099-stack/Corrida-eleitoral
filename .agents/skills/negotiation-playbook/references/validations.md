# Negotiation Playbook - Validations

## Missing BATNA

### **Id**
no-batna
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Negotiator should know their alternatives
### **Message**
Entering negotiation without BATNA analysis.
### **Fix Action**
Identify and develop alternatives before negotiating

## Insufficient Preparation

### **Id**
unprepared-negotiation
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Negotiator should prepare key elements
### **Indicators**
  - No reservation point set
  - Counterparty not researched
### **Message**
Negotiation preparation incomplete.
### **Fix Action**
Complete preparation checklist before negotiating

## Positional Bargaining

### **Id**
position-based
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Should focus on interests not positions
### **Indicators**
  - Trading positions without exploring interests
  - Stuck on specific demands
### **Message**
Negotiating positions instead of interests.
### **Fix Action**
Explore underlying interests of both parties

## Unplanned Concessions

### **Id**
no-concession-plan
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Concessions should be strategic and planned
### **Indicators**
  - Reactive concessions
  - No reciprocity for concessions
### **Message**
Making concessions without strategy.
### **Fix Action**
Plan concession sequence and link to reciprocal asks

## Relationship Neglected

### **Id**
relationship-damage
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Should preserve relationship while negotiating
### **Indicators**
  - Adversarial tone
  - Win-lose framing
### **Message**
Negotiation damaging relationship.
### **Fix Action**
Separate people from problem, focus on mutual gains

## Agreement Not Documented

### **Id**
no-documentation
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Agreements should be written promptly
### **Message**
Verbal agreement without documentation.
### **Fix Action**
Document all agreed terms in writing immediately
