# Research AI Tools

## Patterns


---
  #### **Name**
Start broad, go deep
  #### **Description**
Initial broad search, then drill into specifics
  #### **Example**
What is X? → How does X work? → What are problems with X?
  #### **Impact**
Comprehensive understanding

---
  #### **Name**
Multi-source verification
  #### **Description**
Cross-check claims across tools
  #### **Example**
Perplexity finding → verify in Consensus → primary source
  #### **Impact**
Higher accuracy

---
  #### **Name**
Structured prompts
  #### **Description**
Ask for specific formats
  #### **Example**
Give me 5 bullet points on... with sources
  #### **Impact**
Actionable outputs

---
  #### **Name**
Follow-up chains
  #### **Description**
Build on previous responses
  #### **Example**
Expand on point 3... What about exceptions?
  #### **Impact**
Deeper understanding

## Anti-Patterns


---
  #### **Name**
Trusting without verification
  #### **Description**
Accepting AI claims as fact
  #### **Impact**
Spreading misinformation
  #### **Fix**
Always verify important claims

---
  #### **Name**
Shallow research
  #### **Description**
Stopping at first answer
  #### **Impact**
Missing nuance and context
  #### **Fix**
Follow up, explore edges

---
  #### **Name**
Ignoring citations
  #### **Description**
Not checking sources
  #### **Impact**
Using bad sources
  #### **Fix**
Evaluate source quality

---
  #### **Name**
Over-relying on one tool
  #### **Description**
Only using ChatGPT
  #### **Impact**
Missing specialized insights
  #### **Fix**
Right tool for right task
