# Student Success - Validations

## No Outcome Tracking

### **Id**
no-outcome-tracking
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Should track real student outcomes
### **Indicators**
  - No pre/post surveys
  - Only completion tracked
  - No follow-up after course
### **Message**
Not tracking real student outcomes.
### **Fix Action**
Implement before/after measurement and 30/90 day outcome surveys
