# Student Success

## Patterns


---
  #### **Name**
Outcome Measurement Framework
  #### **Description**
Measuring real student transformation
  #### **When To Use**
When defining and tracking outcomes
  #### **Implementation**
    ## Outcome Measurement Framework
    
    ### Outcome Types
    | Type | Examples | Measurement |
    |------|----------|-------------|
    | Skill | Can do X | Assessment, project |
    | Knowledge | Understands X | Quiz, explanation |
    | Behavior | Does X regularly | Self-report, tracking |
    | Result | Achieved X | Evidence, metrics |
    
    ### The Outcome Stack
    1. **Leading indicators** (during course)
       - Engagement
       - Assignment completion
       - Quiz scores
       - Community participation
    
    2. **Lagging indicators** (after course)
       - Goal achievement
       - Career change
       - Revenue increase
       - Skill application
    
    ### Measurement Timeline
    | When | What to Measure |
    |------|-----------------|
    | Start | Baseline (before state) |
    | During | Progress indicators |
    | End | Immediate outcomes |
    | 30 days | Short-term results |
    | 90 days | Medium-term results |
    | 1 year | Long-term transformation |
    
    ### Outcome Survey Template
    ```
    Pre-course:
    - Where are you now with [skill]? (1-10)
    - What's your goal?
    
    Post-course:
    - Where are you now with [skill]? (1-10)
    - Did you achieve your goal?
    - What specific results?
    - Would you recommend? (NPS)
    ```

  #### **Name**
Testimonial System
  #### **Description**
Collecting and leveraging social proof
  #### **When To Use**
When building testimonial collection
  #### **Implementation**
    ## Testimonial System
    
    ### Testimonial Types
    | Type | Power | Effort |
    |------|-------|--------|
    | Written quote | Medium | Low |
    | Video testimonial | High | Medium |
    | Case study | Highest | High |
    | Social proof (numbers) | Medium | Low |
    
    ### Collection Timing
    | Moment | Ask For |
    |--------|---------|
    | Completion | Quick win quote |
    | 30 days | Results testimonial |
    | 90 days | Transformation story |
    | Success milestone | Case study interview |
    
    ### The Testimonial Ask
    ```
    Subject: Quick favor? (2 min)
    
    Hey [Name],
    
    Congrats on [specific achievement]!
    
    Would you mind sharing a quick testimonial?
    Just 2-3 sentences about:
    - Where you were before
    - What changed
    - Specific results
    
    Here's a form: [link]
    
    Thanks!
    ```
    
    ### Video Testimonial Questions
    1. What were you struggling with before?
    2. What made you join [course]?
    3. What specific results did you get?
    4. What would you tell someone considering it?
    
    ### Case Study Template
    - Background (who they are)
    - Challenge (before state)
    - Solution (what they learned)
    - Results (specific outcomes)
    - Quote (in their words)

  #### **Name**
Certification Design
  #### **Description**
Creating meaningful credentials
  #### **When To Use**
When adding certification to course
  #### **Implementation**
    ## Certification Design
    
    ### Certification Types
    | Type | Best For | Requirements |
    |------|----------|--------------|
    | Completion | Participation proof | Finish course |
    | Achievement | Skill demonstration | Pass assessment |
    | Professional | Industry recognition | Rigorous exam |
    
    ### Requirements Spectrum
    | Level | Rigor | Value |
    |-------|-------|-------|
    | Participation | Low | Low |
    | Quiz-based | Medium | Medium |
    | Project-based | High | High |
    | Peer + instructor review | Highest | Highest |
    
    ### Certificate Elements
    - Student name
    - Course name
    - Date issued
    - Your signature/brand
    - Verification URL
    - Skills covered (optional)
    
    ### Making Certification Valuable
    - Require real demonstration
    - Partner with employers
    - Share on LinkedIn
    - Verification system
    - Ongoing requirements (recertification)
    
    ### Digital Badges
    - Open Badges standard
    - Credly, Badgr platforms
    - LinkedIn integration
    - Shareable and verifiable

## Anti-Patterns


---
  #### **Name**
Vanity Completion Metrics
  #### **Description**
Celebrating completion without outcomes
  #### **Why Bad**
    Completion ≠ transformation.
    Can't prove real value.
    Students may not apply learning.
    No social proof.
    
  #### **What To Do Instead**
    Track actual outcomes.
    Follow up at 30/90 days.
    Measure what they DID.
    Get specific results.
    

---
  #### **Name**
No Testimonial System
  #### **Description**
Hoping testimonials happen organically
  #### **Why Bad**
    Happy students don't think to share.
    Miss the right timing.
    No consistent pipeline.
    Sales page lacks proof.
    
  #### **What To Do Instead**
    Systematic asks at key moments.
    Make it easy.
    Follow up with successful students.
    Incentivize (but keep authentic).
    

---
  #### **Name**
Meaningless Certification
  #### **Description**
Certificate without real requirements
  #### **Why Bad**
    Devalues your brand.
    No real skill verification.
    Employers ignore it.
    Students don't respect it.
    
  #### **What To Do Instead**
    Require real demonstration.
    Assessment or project.
    Make it mean something.
    Better no cert than fake cert.
