# Education Business - Validations

## Building Course Before Audience

### **Id**
no-audience-before-course
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Should have audience before creating course
### **Indicators**
  - No email list
  - Building in isolation
  - No validation of demand
### **Message**
Building course without audience - high risk of launch flop.
### **Fix Action**
Build email list of 500+ interested prospects before creating full course

## No Clear Positioning

### **Id**
no-clear-positioning
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Course should have clear, differentiated positioning
### **Indicators**
  - Generic topic description
  - No specific audience
  - Can't explain why this vs competitors
### **Message**
Course lacks clear positioning.
### **Fix Action**
Define specific audience + unique method/approach that differentiates

## Pricing Not Based on Value

### **Id**
pricing-not-value-based
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Price should reflect outcome value, not content length
### **Indicators**
  - Priced by hours of content
  - Matching competitor prices
  - Imposter syndrome pricing
### **Message**
Pricing not connected to outcome value.
### **Fix Action**
Price based on transformation value and student ROI

## No Launch Runway

### **Id**
no-launch-runway
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Launch should have proper warmup period
### **Indicators**
  - Surprise launch announcement
  - No pre-launch content
  - Cold audience at launch
### **Message**
Launching without proper runway.
### **Fix Action**
Plan 4-6 week warmup with content, waitlist, and anticipation building

## Dependent on Single Product

### **Id**
single-product-dependency
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Business should have multiple revenue streams
### **Indicators**
  - Only one course
  - No upsells or downsells
  - No recurring revenue
### **Message**
Business dependent on single product launches.
### **Fix Action**
Build course ladder and/or recurring revenue stream
