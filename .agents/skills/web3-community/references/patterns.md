# Web3 Community

## Patterns


---
  #### **Name**
Token Community Building
  #### **Description**
Building community around a token
  #### **When To Use**
When managing token holder community
  #### **Implementation**
    ## Token Community Building
    
    ### The Challenge
    Token communities attract:
    - True believers (10%)
    - Speculators (70%)
    - Scammers (20%)
    
    ### Community Value Beyond Price
    | Value Type | Examples |
    |------------|----------|
    | Alpha | Market insights, early info |
    | Education | Learn Web3, trading |
    | Network | Connect with builders |
    | Utility | Product access, governance |
    | Culture | Memes, identity, belonging |
    
    ### Channel Structure
    ```
    📢 Announcements (read-only)
    💬 General Chat
    📊 Price/Trading (contain the speculation)
    🔧 Product/Tech
    🏛️ Governance
    🎨 Memes/Culture
    🆘 Support
    ```
    
    ### Managing Price Discussion
    - Dedicate channel for it (don't ban it)
    - No price predictions from team
    - Redirect speculation to dedicated channel
    - Focus official comms on utility
    
    ### Bear Market Survival
    - Focus on builders, not traders
    - Ship consistently
    - Reduce noise, increase signal
    - Maintain communication cadence
    - Celebrate non-price wins

  #### **Implementation**
    ## NFT Community Management
    
    ### NFT Community Lifecycle
    ```
    Hype → Mint → Reveal → Reality Check → Build or Die
    ```
    
    ### Post-Mint Retention
    | Phase | Focus |
    |-------|-------|
    | Week 1 | Celebrate, build culture |
    | Month 1 | Deliver first utility |
    | Month 3 | Prove long-term vision |
    | Month 6+ | Sustained value delivery |
    
    ### Holder Verification
    - Collab.Land or similar for Discord
    - Token-gated channels
    - Holder-only benefits
    - Tiered access (1 NFT, 5 NFT, etc.)
    
    ### Floor Price Management
    - Never promise floor prices
    - Focus on utility, not speculation
    - Community treasury for buybacks (optional)
    - Transparent about market conditions
    
    ### Culture Building
    - PFP culture (encourage use)
    - Derivative art celebration
    - Holder spotlights
    - IRL meetups
    - Shared identity

  #### **Name**
DAO Community Governance
  #### **Description**
Managing community-driven governance
  #### **When To Use**
When building or managing DAO
  #### **Implementation**
    ## DAO Community Governance
    
    ### Governance Structure
    | Type | Best For |
    |------|----------|
    | Token voting | Large decisions |
    | Delegate system | Participation at scale |
    | Multisig | Treasury, execution |
    | Working groups | Day-to-day operations |
    
    ### Proposal Process
    1. Temp check (gauge interest)
    2. RFC (request for comments)
    3. Formal proposal
    4. Voting period
    5. Execution
    
    ### Preventing Governance Fatigue
    - Don't vote on everything
    - Delegate system
    - Clear scope for each level
    - Compensate active participants
    - Async-friendly processes
    
    ### Governance Attacks
    | Attack | Prevention |
    |--------|------------|
    | Vote buying | Time-locked voting |
    | Flash loans | Snapshot at proposal |
    | Plutocracy | Quadratic voting |
    | Apathy | Quorum requirements |

  #### **Name**
Crypto Twitter Strategy
  #### **Description**
Building Web3 presence on Twitter/X
  #### **When To Use**
When building Web3 Twitter presence
  #### **Implementation**
    ## Crypto Twitter Strategy
    
    ### CT Culture
    - Memes are currency
    - Speed matters
    - Hot takes get engagement
    - Thread game is essential
    - Spaces for thought leadership
    
    ### Content Types
    | Type | Frequency | Purpose |
    |------|-----------|---------|
    | Alpha threads | 2x/week | Establish expertise |
    | Memes | Daily | Culture, virality |
    | Project updates | As needed | Inform holders |
    | Spaces | Weekly | Thought leadership |
    
    ### Engagement Tactics
    - Quote tweet with takes
    - Reply to relevant conversations
    - Support other projects (genuine)
    - Raid sparingly, authentically
    - Collab with other accounts
    
    ### What to Avoid
    - Shilling constantly
    - Engagement farming
    - Attacking competitors
    - Price predictions
    - Fake alpha
    

## Anti-Patterns


---
  #### **Name**
Promising Price
  #### **Description**
Making price predictions or promises
  #### **Why Bad**
    Securities law issues.
    Attracts wrong crowd.
    Impossible to deliver.
    Destroys trust when price drops.
    
  #### **What To Do Instead**
    Focus on utility.
    Talk about product.
    Let price take care of itself.
    Build for believers, not traders.
    

---
  #### **Name**
Rug Pull Patterns
  #### **Description**
Behaviors that look like exit scam setup
  #### **Why Bad**
    Even unintentional, these destroy trust.
    Community assumes the worst.
    Hard to recover from perception.
    
  #### **What To Do Instead**
    Transparent team (doxxed or proven track record).
    Clear roadmap with delivered milestones.
    Treasury transparency.
    Long-term vesting.
    Consistent communication.
    

---
  #### **Name**
Mercenary Community
  #### **Description**
Community only there for gains
  #### **Why Bad**
    Leaves when price drops.
    No genuine connection.
    Toxic culture.
    No long-term value.
    
  #### **What To Do Instead**
    Build utility beyond speculation.
    Create genuine culture.
    Filter for believers in onboarding.
    Provide non-financial value.
