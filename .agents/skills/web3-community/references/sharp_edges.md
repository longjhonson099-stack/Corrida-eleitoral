# Web3 Community - Sharp Edges

## Scam Exploitation

### **Id**
scam-exploitation
### **Summary**
Scammers targeting your community
### **Severity**
critical
### **Situation**
Community members losing funds to scams
### **Why**
  Web3 communities are scammer magnets.
  DMs, fake airdrops, phishing sites.
  Your community's reputation suffers.
  
### **Solution**
  ## Anti-Scam Protocols
  
  ### Education (Constant)
  - Pin: "We will NEVER DM you first"
  - Pin: "We will NEVER ask for seed phrase"
  - Regular reminders
  - Scam example screenshots
  
  ### Technical Prevention
  | Platform | Tools |
  |----------|-------|
  | Discord | Wick, Carl-bot, disable DMs |
  | Telegram | Anti-spam bots, slow mode |
  | Twitter | Verified checkmark |
  
  ### When Scam Happens
  1. Warn community immediately
  2. Document the scam
  3. Report to platform
  4. Share on Twitter (awareness)
  5. Support affected members
  6. Never blame victims
  
  ### Proactive Measures
  - Claim similar domain names
  - Monitor for fake accounts
  - Trademark protection
  - Regular security reminders
  
### **Symptoms**
  - I got a DM from admin
  - Phishing links shared
  - Fake token launches
  - Impersonator accounts
### **Detection Pattern**
scam|hack|stole|fake|impersonator|lost funds

## Bear Market Death

### **Id**
bear-market-death
### **Summary**
Community dies in bear market
### **Severity**
high
### **Situation**
Activity drops 90%+ when prices crash
### **Why**
  Speculators leave.
  Team loses motivation.
  Community loses faith.
  Vicious cycle.
  
### **Solution**
  ## Bear Market Survival Guide
  
  ### Expect It
  - Don't build for bull only
  - Reserve funds for 2+ years
  - Plan for 90% activity drop
  
  ### Retention Strategies
  | Strategy | Implementation |
  |----------|----------------|
  | Ship faster | Bear = build time |
  | Core community | Focus on believers |
  | Utility focus | Non-price value |
  | Reduce noise | Fewer channels, more signal |
  
  ### Communication
  - Maintain cadence (don't go quiet)
  - Honest about market conditions
  - Share roadmap progress
  - Celebrate non-price wins
  
  ### Team Morale
  - Acknowledge difficulty
  - Adjust expectations
  - Focus on controllables
  - Small wins matter
  
  ### Who Stays
  - True believers
  - Utility users
  - Community contributors
  - Builders
  
  ### Silver Lining
  - Best time to build
  - Less noise
  - Real community revealed
  - Talent available
  
### **Symptoms**
  - Chat goes quiet
  - Holders selling
  - Negative sentiment
  - Team burnout
### **Detection Pattern**
dead|quiet|bear|selling|leaving|wen moon

## Governance Attack

### **Id**
governance-attack
### **Summary**
Malicious actors exploit governance
### **Severity**
critical
### **Situation**
Bad actors manipulate DAO votes
### **Why**
  Token-weighted voting is exploitable.
  Flash loan attacks.
  Vote buying.
  Coordinated action.
  
### **Solution**
  ## Governance Security
  
  ### Prevention
  - Snapshot voting (block before proposal)
  - Time-locked tokens
  - Quorum requirements
  - Proposal thresholds
  - Delegate system
  
  ### Monitoring
  - Watch large token movements
  - New wallets accumulating
  - Unusual proposal patterns
  - Coordinated voting
  
  ### Response
  1. Pause governance if possible
  2. Investigate the attack
  3. Community communication
  4. Fix vulnerability
  5. Consider rollback if needed
  
  ### Healthy Governance
  - Multiple safeguards
  - Not everything is voteable
  - Emergency multisig
  - Transparent processes
  
### **Symptoms**
  - Unusual proposals
  - Large token movements
  - Coordinated voting
  - Hostile takeover attempts
### **Detection Pattern**
governance attack|vote manipulation|hostile|takeover

## Regulatory Crackdown

### **Id**
regulatory-crackdown
### **Summary**
Regulatory action against project/community
### **Severity**
critical
### **Situation**
SEC, CFTC, or other regulatory scrutiny
### **Why**
  Unclear regulatory environment.
  Securities law questions.
  Jurisdiction issues.
  
### **Solution**
  ## Regulatory Preparedness
  
  ### Prevention
  - Legal review of token structure
  - Avoid securities language
  - No profit promises
  - Geographic restrictions
  - Clear terms of service
  
  ### Communication Don'ts
  - Don't promise returns
  - Don't call tokens investments
  - Don't guarantee price
  - Don't compare to stocks
  
  ### If Contacted by Regulators
  1. Engage legal counsel immediately
  2. Don't destroy evidence
  3. Cooperate appropriately
  4. Communicate carefully to community
  5. Don't speculate publicly
  
  ### Community Communication
  - Be factual
  - Don't spread FUD
  - Follow legal guidance
  - Maintain transparency within limits
  
### **Symptoms**
  - Subpoena received
  - Regulatory statements
  - Legal uncertainty
  - Exchange delistings
### **Detection Pattern**
SEC|regulation|securities|legal action|subpoena
