# Synthetic Influencers

## Patterns


---
  #### **Name**
Persona Bible Creation
  #### **Description**
Define every aspect of a synthetic influencer's identity
  #### **When**
Creating a new synthetic influencer
  #### **Example**
    PERSONA BIBLE STRUCTURE:
    
    1. IDENTITY
       - Name and origin story
       - Age, background, "history"
       - Role in brand world
       - Relationship to brand (why do they exist?)
    
    2. VISUAL IDENTITY
       - Character design (see ai-world-building)
       - Signature looks and variations
       - Settings where they appear
       - Visual evolution plan
    
    3. PERSONALITY
       - Core traits (3-5 defining characteristics)
       - Values and beliefs
       - Interests and passions
       - Fears and challenges
       - Sense of humor
       - Controversial takes (that are brand-safe)
    
    4. VOICE AND LANGUAGE
       - Speaking style
       - Vocabulary patterns
       - Phrases they use
       - Phrases they NEVER use
       - Emoji style
       - Caption structure
    
    5. CONTENT THEMES
       - Topics they talk about
       - Topics they avoid
       - Content formats they use
       - Posting frequency
       - Engagement style
    
    6. RELATIONSHIPS
       - With brand (explicit or subtle?)
       - With audience (how they address followers)
       - With other characters (if applicable)
    
    7. GROWTH ARC
       - How will they evolve?
       - Planned milestones
       - Narrative developments
    

---
  #### **Name**
The Authenticity Paradox Framework
  #### **Description**
Create genuine connection through acknowledged artificiality
  #### **When**
Developing synthetic influencer strategy
  #### **Example**
    THE PARADOX:
    Audiences know synthetic influencers aren't real—and connect anyway.
    Why? Because they offer something different, not fake-real.
    
    AUTHENTICITY MOVES:
    
    1. TRANSPARENT ARTIFICIALITY:
       - Bio clearly states AI/virtual nature
       - Never pretend to be human
       - Lean into being synthetic
    
    2. CONSISTENT PERSONALITY:
       - Real personalities are inconsistent
       - Synthetic can be perfectly consistent
       - Consistency IS the authenticity
    
    3. UNIQUE PERSPECTIVE:
       - "As an AI, I see this differently..."
       - Commentary on being synthetic
       - Meta-awareness
    
    4. GENUINE VALUE:
       - Entertainment
       - Information
       - Community
       - Something real even if source isn't
    
    5. EMOTIONAL HONESTY:
       - AI expressing "feelings" about things
       - Not claiming to feel like humans
       - But having perspectives and preferences
    
    RULE: Don't try to pass as human. Succeed as interesting.
    

---
  #### **Name**
Content Velocity Strategy
  #### **Description**
Leverage synthetic advantage for content volume
  #### **When**
Planning content strategy for synthetic influencer
  #### **Example**
    SYNTHETIC ADVANTAGE: NO HUMAN CONSTRAINTS
    
    Content velocity human influencer can't match:
    - Daily posts across platforms
    - Responses at any hour
    - Multiple simultaneous campaigns
    - No travel/scheduling limitations
    - No "off days" or sick days
    
    VELOCITY STRATEGY:
    
    DAILY CONTENT:
    - 1 main post (image + caption)
    - 3-5 story frames
    - Comment responses
    - Generated in batch, scheduled
    
    WEEKLY CONTENT:
    - 1 video (digital human)
    - 1 collaboration/feature
    - Community engagement focus
    
    MONTHLY CONTENT:
    - Campaign integration
    - Narrative developments
    - Character growth moments
    
    PRODUCTION:
    - Batch generate weekly content in one session
    - Schedule using tools
    - Monitor and respond (can be AI-assisted)
    - Maintain consistency with persona bible
    

---
  #### **Name**
Multi-Platform Presence
  #### **Description**
Adapt synthetic influencer across platforms
  #### **When**
Expanding synthetic influencer presence
  #### **Example**
    PLATFORM ADAPTATION:
    
    INSTAGRAM:
    - Primary visual platform
    - Lifestyle imagery
    - Stories for personality
    - Reels for reach
    
    TIKTOK:
    - More casual, raw aesthetic
    - Trend participation
    - Behind-the-scenes "creator" content
    - Can acknowledge synthetic nature playfully
    
    YOUTUBE:
    - Long-form personality content
    - Digital human video
    - Q&A, day-in-the-life style
    - Deepest personality expression
    
    TWITTER/X:
    - Voice and opinions
    - Hot takes (brand-safe)
    - Conversation and engagement
    - Real-time presence
    
    DISCORD:
    - Community hub
    - Direct interaction
    - Loyal fan space
    - Can use AI chat for presence
    
    RULE: Adapt to platform norms, but maintain core personality.
    Synthetic influencer should feel like same person everywhere.
    

---
  #### **Name**
Crisis-Proof by Design
  #### **Description**
Build synthetic influencers to avoid controversy
  #### **When**
Designing synthetic influencer strategy
  #### **Example**
    CRISIS IMMUNITY FEATURES:
    
    1. CONTENT CONTROL:
       - Every post is reviewed before publishing
       - No off-the-cuff mistakes
       - No personal scandals
       - No embarrassing old posts (you control the archive)
    
    2. TOPIC BOUNDARIES:
       - Define topics in persona bible
       - Automated flag for boundary violations
       - Clear lanes for what character discusses
    
    3. CONTROVERSY STRATEGY:
       - Persona can have opinions
       - But opinions are designed
       - No surprises
    
    4. EVOLUTION CONTROL:
       - Character growth is planned
       - No unexpected changes
       - Narrative arcs are approved
    
    5. CANCELATION RESPONSE:
       - If synthetic influencer somehow gets "canceled"
       - Can evolve, address, or retire gracefully
       - No human livelihood at stake
    
    HOWEVER:
    - Brand can still be criticized for synthetic influencer actions
    - Must still be ethical, inclusive, respectful
    - Synthetic doesn't mean unaccountable
    

## Anti-Patterns


---
  #### **Name**
Hiding Synthetic Nature
  #### **Description**
Not clearly disclosing that influencer is AI-generated
  #### **Why**
Destroys trust when discovered; potentially illegal (FTC)
  #### **Instead**
Transparent in bio. Proud of being synthetic.

---
  #### **Name**
Uncanny Valley Push
  #### **Description**
Trying to make synthetic influencer too human-like
  #### **Why**
The closer to human, the more disturbing the flaws
  #### **Instead**
Stylized, clearly synthetic, or perfectly photorealistic. Not between.

---
  #### **Name**
Personality Drift
  #### **Description**
Inconsistent personality across content
  #### **Why**
Breaks the illusion; audiences notice inconsistency
  #### **Instead**
Persona bible. Style guide. Review process.

---
  #### **Name**
All Promotion, No Value
  #### **Description**
Synthetic influencer that only shills products
  #### **Why**
Audiences don't follow ads; they follow interesting entities
  #### **Instead**
Entertainment/value first. Brand integration naturally.

---
  #### **Name**
Ignoring Community
  #### **Description**
Posting without genuine engagement
  #### **Why**
Synthetic influencers still need community building
  #### **Instead**
Respond, engage, acknowledge. Human-assisted community management.

---
  #### **Name**
Static Character
  #### **Description**
Influencer that never grows or changes
  #### **Why**
Real-feeling entities evolve; static feels dead
  #### **Instead**
Planned evolution arcs. Character growth. New developments.
