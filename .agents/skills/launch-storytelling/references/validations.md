# Launch Storytelling - Validations

## Missing Launch Story

### **Id**
no-launch-narrative
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Launch must have clear narrative, not just feature list
### **Indicators**
  - Launch content is only feature list
  - No story arc or hook
### **Message**
Launch lacks compelling narrative.
### **Fix Action**
Create launch story with hook, problem, solution, and call to action

## No Post-Launch Content

### **Id**
no-post-launch-plan
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Launch should have 7-14 days of follow-up content planned
### **Message**
Launch has no post-launch content plan.
### **Fix Action**
Plan 2 weeks of post-launch content to sustain momentum

## Single Channel Launch

### **Id**
single-channel
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Launch should span multiple channels
### **Indicators**
  - Only announcing on one platform
  - No email component
### **Message**
Launch limited to single channel.
### **Fix Action**
Plan multi-channel launch: email, social, blog, PR

## Overpromising in Launch Messaging

### **Id**
overpromise-messaging
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Launch claims should be provable
### **Indicators**
  - Superlatives without proof
  - Unverifiable claims
### **Message**
Launch messaging overpromises.
