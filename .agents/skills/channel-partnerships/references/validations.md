# Channel Partnerships - Validations

## Missing Partner Economics Model

### **Id**
no-partner-economics
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Partner program must have clear economics defined
### **Message**
Partner program lacks defined margins and economics.
### **Fix Action**
Define margin structure, incentives, and profitability targets

## No Partner Activation Criteria

### **Id**
no-activation-requirements
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Partner program must define activation milestones
### **Indicators**
  - Partners signed without activation plan
  - No onboarding milestones
### **Message**
Partners can be signed without activation requirements.
### **Fix Action**
Define activation milestones: training, certification, first deal

## Missing Deal Registration System

### **Id**
