# Learning Experience - Validations

## Lessons Too Long

### **Id**
lessons-too-long
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Lessons should be under 12 minutes
### **Indicators**
  - Videos over 15 minutes
  - No chapter markers
  - Multiple concepts per video
### **Message**
Lessons are too long - hurts completion.
### **Fix Action**
Break lessons into 5-10 minute chunks with one concept each

## No Progress Indicators

### **Id**
no-progress-indicators
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Students should see their progress
### **Indicators**
  - No progress bar
  - No completion tracking
  - No milestone celebrations
### **Message**
Course lacks progress visibility.
### **Fix Action**
Add progress bar, completion checkmarks, and milestone celebrations

## No Interaction Points

### **Id**
no-interaction-points
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Content should have regular interaction
### **Indicators**
  - Long passive videos
  - No quizzes or exercises
  - No pause-and-try moments
### **Message**
Content lacks interaction points.
### **Fix Action**
Add knowledge checks every 2-3 lessons and exercises per module

## Single Content Modality

### **Id**
single-modality
### **Severity**
low
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Should use multiple learning formats
### **Indicators**
  - Video only, no text
  - Text only, no visuals
  - No downloadable resources
### **Message**
Course relies on single content format.
### **Fix Action**
Add complementary formats: text summaries, audio, worksheets

## Not Mobile Optimized

### **Id**
no-mobile-optimization
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Course should work on mobile devices
### **Indicators**
  - Small text in videos
  - Flash or non-mobile tech
  - No mobile testing
### **Message**
Course not optimized for mobile learners.
### **Fix Action**
Test on mobile devices, optimize video text size, ensure responsive design
