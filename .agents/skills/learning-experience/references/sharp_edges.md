# Learning Experience - Sharp Edges

## Completion Crisis

### **Id**
completion-crisis
### **Summary**
Course has very low completion rates
### **Severity**
high
### **Situation**
< 20% of students complete the course
### **Why**
  Lessons too long.
  No momentum mechanics.
  No accountability.
  Poor onboarding.
  
### **Solution**
  ## Fixing Low Completion
  
  ### Diagnose Drop-off Points
  1. Pull completion data by lesson
  2. Identify biggest drop-offs
  3. Analyze those specific lessons
  
  ### Quick Fixes
  | Issue | Fix |
  |-------|-----|
  | Long lessons | Split into 5-7 min chunks |
  | No progress | Add visual progress bar |
  | No urgency | Add optional deadlines |
  | No hook | Improve first lesson |
  
  ### Structural Fixes
  - Shorten overall course (cut 30%)
  - Add checkpoints/quizzes
  - Implement email sequences
  - Create peer accountability
  
  ### First Week Focus
  Students who complete week 1 are 4x more likely to finish.
  - Optimize first 3 lessons
  - Early win by day 3
  - Personal welcome message
  - Day 7 check-in email
  
### **Symptoms**
  - 80%+ students don't finish
  - Drop-off after lesson 1-3
  - Low activity after first week
  - Students say "I'll come back to it"
### **Detection Pattern**
didn't finish|haven't completed|dropped off|stopped

## Boring Content

### **Id**
boring-content
### **Summary**
Students find course boring or dry
### **Severity**
medium
### **Situation**
Low engagement despite good curriculum
### **Why**
  Talking head syndrome.
  No variety in format.
  Missing emotional connection.
  Too academic/formal.
  
### **Solution**
  ## Making Content Engaging
  
  ### The Engagement Audit
  Rate each lesson:
  - Visual variety (1-5)
  - Story/example use (1-5)
  - Interaction points (1-5)
  - Energy level (1-5)
  
  ### Quick Engagement Wins
  | Technique | Effect |
  |-----------|--------|
  | Add b-roll | Visual interest |
  | Personal stories | Emotional connection |
  | Change scenery | Pattern interrupt |
  | Student examples | Relevance |
  | Humor (careful) | Dopamine |
  
  ### Format Variety
  - Mix talking head + screen share
  - Add diagrams and animations
  - Include student case studies
  - Use guest experts
  - Add "behind the scenes"
  
  ### The 3-Minute Rule
  Every 3 minutes, change something:
  - Camera angle
  - Visual on screen
  - Tone/energy
  - Question/interaction
  
### **Symptoms**
  - Low time-on-task
  - Feedback mentions "dry" or "boring"
  - Students skip ahead
  - Low completion of optional content
### **Detection Pattern**
boring|dry|skipped|couldn't focus|hard to watch

## Gamification Backfire

### **Id**
gamification-backfire
### **Summary**
Gamification creates wrong behaviors
### **Severity**
medium
### **Situation**
Students gaming the system, not learning
### **Why**
  Wrong behaviors rewarded.
  Points without purpose.
  Leaderboards discourage.
  Extrinsic overtaking intrinsic.
  
### **Solution**
  ## Healthy Gamification
  
  ### Signs of Backfire
  - Racing through content for points
  - Checking in without engaging
  - Quitting when streak breaks
  - Comparing and feeling bad
  
  ### Fixing Points Systems
  | Instead of... | Reward... |
  |---------------|-----------|
  | Completion speed | Genuine engagement |
  | Quantity | Quality (assessed) |
  | Daily login | Meaningful progress |
  | Beating others | Personal bests |
  
  ### Leaderboard Alternatives
  - Progress bars (personal)
  - Milestone celebrations
  - Class progress (anonymized)
  - Opt-in competition
  
  ### Streak Recovery
  - Allow 1 skip/week
  - "Streak freeze" option
  - Weekend doesn't count
  - Focus on personal records
  
### **Symptoms**
  - Students rushing through
  - Demotivation from leaderboards
  - Frustration at lost streaks
  - Gaming quiz attempts
### **Detection Pattern**
lost my streak|gave up|too far behind|just clicking through

## Mobile Disaster

### **Id**
mobile-disaster
### **Summary**
Course doesn't work on mobile
### **Severity**
medium
### **Situation**
Mobile users have poor experience
### **Why**
  Designed for desktop only.
  Small text unreadable.
  Interactions don't work.
  Videos won't load.
  
### **Solution**
  ## Mobile Optimization
  
  ### Mobile Audit Checklist
  - [ ] Videos play without issues
  - [ ] Text readable without zoom
  - [ ] Buttons easily tappable
  - [ ] Progress saves across devices
  - [ ] Downloads for offline
  - [ ] Loads on 3G
  
  ### Design Rules
  | Desktop | Mobile Adaptation |
  |---------|-------------------|
  | 16px text | 18px minimum |
  | Click targets | 44x44px minimum |
  | Hover states | Tap alternatives |
  | Side-by-side | Stacked layout |
  
  ### Video for Mobile
  - Offer vertical option
  - Larger text on slides
  - Audio-only alternative
  - Captions (read in transit)
  - Lower resolution option
  
  ### Platform Check
  - Test on iPhone + Android
  - Test on tablet
  - Test on slow connection
  - Test offline mode
  
### **Symptoms**
  - Mobile users drop off faster
  - Can't see on my phone
  - Support tickets about mobile
  - Low mobile engagement
### **Detection Pattern**
phone|mobile|can't see|too small|doesn't work
