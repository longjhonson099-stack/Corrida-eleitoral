## Thumbnail Exploration Checklist

### **Id**
thumbnail-exploration
### **Phase**
exploration
### **Description**
Validates proper ideation process before refinement
### **Checklist**
  
---
    ##### **Id**
quantity
    ##### **Check**
Minimum 30 thumbnails created (50+ preferred)
    ##### **Required**

    ##### **Rationale**
Ensures adequate exploration of solution space
  
---
    ##### **Id**
variety
    ##### **Check**
Thumbnails show meaningfully different directions, not iterations of same idea
    ##### **Required**

    ##### **Rationale**
Prevents premature fixation on single solution
  
---
    ##### **Id**
time-boxing
    ##### **Check**
Each thumbnail took 1-3 minutes maximum
    ##### **Required**

    ##### **Rationale**
Forces big-picture thinking, prevents detail trap
  
---
    ##### **Id**
size-constraint
    ##### **Check**
Thumbnails are 2-3 inches, not larger
    ##### **Required**

    ##### **Rationale**
Small size enforces value/shape thinking over detail
  
---
    ##### **Id**
numbered
    ##### **Check**
All thumbnails numbered for easy reference in reviews
    ##### **Required**

    ##### **Rationale**
Enables clear communication with art director
  
---
    ##### **Id**
extreme-exploration
    ##### **Check**
Includes at least 3 'extreme' options (biggest, smallest, weirdest)
    ##### **Required**

    ##### **Rationale**
Pushes creative boundaries, often yields best ideas
  
---
    ##### **Id**
squint-test
    ##### **Check**
Selected thumbnails pass squint test for value readability
    ##### **Required**

    ##### **Rationale**
Ensures composition works at fundamental level
