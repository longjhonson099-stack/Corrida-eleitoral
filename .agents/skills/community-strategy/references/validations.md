# Community Strategy - Validations

## Community Without Clear Purpose

### **Id**
missing-community-purpose
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Community should have documented purpose
### **Indicators**
  - No mission statement
  - Unclear why community exists
  - Can't articulate value prop
### **Message**
Community lacks clear, documented purpose.
### **Fix Action**
Define and document community mission, vision, and value proposition
